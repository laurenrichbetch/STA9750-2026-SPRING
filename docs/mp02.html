WRITE UP

As people get older they spend more time on household activities - this makes sense as the older community (60+) is largely going to be retired and spend more time at home versus younger demographics, who are working and out of the house. It’s also possible that the same tasks that used to be completed in a shorter amount of time now take longer as the older demographics do not move as quickly as they once had - they would also need to be mindful of moving in a way that would prevent injuries.

wRITE UP

During the summer months, people who work in Education spend most of their time on personal care, with Socializing, Relaxing and Leisure as a close second. This makes sense as working in education during the school year requires early wake up times, and lots of energy – thus, making the summer the perfect opportunity to relax, refresh and reset.

WRITE UP

The amount of time spent on educational activities is comparable across age groups.

WRITE UP

It is unsurprising that participation in water sports significantly decreases as people age – water sports require people to be very fit, as it can often mean treading water for extended periods of time – as people age, they lose muscle mass, and therefore, it can be dangerous to partake in a competitive sport in the water. Likewise, weight-lifting and/or strength-training also skews young, as it can be challenging and unsafe to lift heavy weights for older demographics. More people play golf as they continue to age as it’s not a strenuous sport. Walking and fishing trend flat across demos as these are also low-impact sports/ activities.

WRITE UP

Time per child decreases as the number of children in the household increases – this makes so much sense because each parent only has a set amount of hours in the day, and as more children enter the household, there is simply less time to be spent on each one of time.
