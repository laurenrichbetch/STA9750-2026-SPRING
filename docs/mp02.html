Activity 6 - Graph #1

Write up

Overall finding: As people get older, they generally end up spending more time on household chores. Additional insights: Younger adults usually do the least — often because they’re living in smaller places or don’t have as many responsibilities yet. The time commitment grows through midlife, when things like taking care of a home or family start to pile up. For folks 60 and over, the amount of time spent on household tasks peaks - these people are likely retired and move slower for the same tasks that took less time to accomplish in their youth!
